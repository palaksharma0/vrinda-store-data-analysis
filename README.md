# 🛍️ Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis 2022

## 📌 Project Overview
Performed complete data analysis on Vrinda Store's 2022 sales data using Microsoft Excel.
Dataset contains **31,000+ orders** with total revenue of **₹2.1 Crore**.

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 👩 Women contribute ~69% of total revenue
- 🎯 Adults (30–49 yrs) = ~48% of orders  
- 🏆 Top States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh (~35% revenue)
- 📱 Amazon, Myntra & Flipkart = ~80% of total sales
- 👗 Sets & Kurtas are highest-selling categories
- ✅ 92.3% order delivery success rate

## 💡 Business Recommendation
Target **women aged 30–49** in **Maharashtra, Karnataka & UP** via promotions on **Amazon, Flipkart & Myntra**.
